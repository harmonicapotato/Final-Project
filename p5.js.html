Self Portrait

For this Self Portrait using P5.js, I created a simplified character that somewhat resembles me but leaves space for imagination. Through the color palette, I hope to capture my personality using soft-edged shapes to make it appear friendly and approachable, like another version of myself.

Interactive Drawing Tool

click and drag to begin drawing !

For this project, since it is an interactive art piece, i decided to create a drawing tool that resembles embroidery and stitched lines. This is inspired by the handmade stitched designs on my cultural clothing. I wanted to appreciate the beauty and art of the cultural patterns.
