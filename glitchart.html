Glitch Art

To me, this project allowed me to experiment with glitching images and getting used to the imperfections that were being created while also being able to appreciate the beauty that is being born.

For my first image, I used a photograph of flowers to help get a better of understanding of the perception of beauty, exploring the idea of how our perception of beauty is distorted by the digital world.

For my second image, I decided to explore the idea of cultural identity, manipulating the image to try and convey the story of "holding on to your heritage/cultural identity".

For my last image, I used my favorite K-pop idols, ENHYPEN, blurring their faces, making it dream-like, as a metaphor for distance, "how much do we really know about our idols?"
