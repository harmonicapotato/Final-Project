3D Sculpture

For this project I was inspired by one of the digital media artist that I was learning more about, 'Shin Oh' . I wanted to experiment in creating a space where it creates the sense of comfort and familiarity. The space is created inspired by the layout of my own bedroom, transforming a real environment into a digital environment. Through this work, I wanted to explore everyday spaces transformed into engaging digital experiences.
