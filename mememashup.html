MEME Mashup

For this project, I thought it would be relatable to make the content around school and class. I'm sure many students feel this way too so I wanted to incorporate how I felt as well. Since this semester is my most packed semester yet, the beginning of the semester feels very draining, and I wanted to capture that sense of exhaustion in my work. By doing so, I hope I am able to express not only my experience but also a feeling many others might connect to.
